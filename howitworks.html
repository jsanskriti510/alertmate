How AlertMate Works

Simple • Smart • Reliable Alert System

Working Process

1
Device Activation
AlertMate is turned on and starts monitoring continuously to ensure your safety from the moment you wear it.
2
Data Collection
High-precision sensors like the accelerometer and gyroscope collect real-time motion data 24/7.
3
Movement Detection
The system differentiates between daily activities and sudden impact. Normal movements are ignored, while high-velocity changes trigger the next phase.
4
Validation
Sophisticated algorithms analyze the impact. If it's a false alarm (like dropping the device), it is ignored. If a real fall is confirmed, the alert sequence begins.
5
Alert Transmission
Once a fall is validated, an emergency signal is instantly sent to your emergency contacts or caregivers with your location data.
