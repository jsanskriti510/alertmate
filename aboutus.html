How it works?

Who We Are?

Our project introduces an integrated IoT-based solution designed to enhance the safety and health of individuals living independently. By combining Real time Fall Detection with an Automated Medicine Reminder, this system addresses two of the most critical challenges in elderly care: physical accidents and medication non-compliance. Our goal is to provide a 'digital companion' that ensures immediate emergency response through SMS alerts while promoting better health through timely medical alerts

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a comprehensive "digital companion" that empowers individuals to live independently by addressing the two most critical risks in elderly care: physical accidents and medication non-compliance. By integrating IoT-based real-time fall detection with automated medicine reminders, we aim to eliminate the dangerous "long-lie" period after a fall through instant emergency SMS alerts. We are committed to making life-saving technology accessible and affordable for all, including lower-income families, by removing recurring subscription fees and utilizing cost-effective, high-quality components.

What We Do?

Our emergency band is equipped with intelligent sensors that detect sudden falls and unusual movements. Once detected, the device sends real-time alerts, ensuring quick response during critical moments.

    We believe life-saving technology should be available to everyone. Unlike many commercial competitors, AlertMate is built on a foundation of affordability:
  • No Subscription Fees:

  • Low Initial Investment:
    • By using high-quality, affordable components, we have created a prototype that is accessible to lower-income families at a fraction of the cost of commercial devices.
