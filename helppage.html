Frequently Asked Questions

What is AlertMate?
AlertMate is a wearable device that detects falls and instantly sends alerts to caregivers or family members, helping ensure quick assistance when every second counts.
How does AlertMate work?
Using advanced motion sensors, it monitors for sudden impacts. If a fall is detected and not dismissed by the user, it automatically triggers notifications through our secure cloud system.
Is AlertMate free to use?
AlertMate costs approximately ₹4000 as a one-time hardware purchase. The best part? There are no monthly subscription fees for our standard alert services.
Can I customize alerts?
Absolutely! Via the companion app, you can set contact priorities, timing delays, and choose between SMS, call, or app notification triggers.
